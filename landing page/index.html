A gym is a dedicated space designed for physical fitness and exercise. With amenities like locker rooms, showers, and sometimes a sauna or pool, gyms cater to diverse fitness needs and promote overall health and well-being. Whether for strength training, cardio, or flexibility, gyms support an active lifestyle and fitness goals.
